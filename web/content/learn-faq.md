## What are the list of reserved keywords in `js`, `nuxt` and `vue`?

> The words that you cannot use.

- `<n-link>`, `<nuxt-link>`, `<router-link>`
- `<client-only>`, `<no-ssr>`
- `<nuxt>`, `<nuxt-child>`
- `<slot>`, `<template>`
- `delete`
